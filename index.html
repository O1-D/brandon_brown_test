Brandon Brown

Lead Generation Expert for Law Firms and Tech Companies at Self Employed

From lead origination to intake, Brandon is an expert in this field for over 20 years. Before starting his own business in 2016, Brandon worked with Pogust, Braslow, and Millrood developing their lead generation and internal intake department. Leveraging his experience, he focused on high quality lead generation, immediate intake response, effective client communication, and technology achieving a 26.8% lead-to-client conversion to secure thousands of clients.

Currently, Brandon focuses a majority of his efforts on lead generation. He works with the largest recognized mass tort and class action law firms and the sole practitioners. Flexible, efficient, and easy to work with, Brandon is always interested in new projects.

Whatever the case is, Brandon can deliver the plaintiff you are looking for, including discreet marketing for the tightly kept litigations building up a plaintiff pool before it becomes public.

To reach Brandon, call 240-462-5858 or email at brandon@brandonbrown.attorney to discuss your project.
